About me



Over my many years, I have found myself. The things that make me tick, the values I hold, and what makes me strong. From the place I started to wherever I end, I am a maker in marketing.

I love to make and understand how making can be applied to brands. From a concept to a prototype, prototype to story, story to experience, experience to psychology of interacting with such experience. One is related to the other and the full thinking is what I provide.

I am a maker, marketer, story teller, problem solver, experimenter, skill collector, entrepreneur, husband, father, and a life long learner.
